Lasagna

Description:

Classic Italian lasagna with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Combine ground beef and tomato sauce in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Layer cooked noodles, meat sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.
