Aussie Chicken Parmigiana

Description:

A classic Aussie pub meal featuring breaded chicken breast topped with tomato sauce and melted cheese, served with chips and salad.

Ingredients:
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F (190Â°C).
  2. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  3. Dredge chicken in beaten eggs, then coat with breadcrumbs and Parmesan.
  4. Fry chicken in oil until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Layer chicken, tomato sauce, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.
