Description:

A perfectly cooked pub-style steak served with chips and a fresh salad.

Ingredients:
  • 2 ribeye steaks (about 1 inch thick)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary or thyme
  • 2 tbsp butter
Instructions:
  1. Season steaks with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a cast iron skillet over high heat.
  3. Add steaks and cook for 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare.
  4. Add garlic, rosemary/thyme, and butter to the pan.
  5. Baste the steaks with the melted butter for 1 minute.
  6. Remove from heat and let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
